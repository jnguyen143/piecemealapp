Welcome to PieceMeal
Welcome to pieceMeal! Your one stop destination to rediscover your appetite. What makes us special? We make cooking an activity to stay connected amidst the pandemic, while offering new and innovative recipes to fit your lifestyle. When you're ready, click the button below to discover all that we have to offer. Enjoy!

The functionality of this app includes but is not limited to searching for recipes by ingredients, filtering by diets, intolerances, cuisines and prep-time. This app also allows users to make profiles and find their friends. Finding friends allows you to see what they have saved and save it to your own profile. Users can also save recipes and ingredients to their profile and pieceMeal will return recommended recipes, based on what they have saved.
